About

Librarian to web design – it’s all in the detail. After years of teaching technology classes, building sites with students and hanging out in school libraries, I decided to pull it all together and build some sites for other people. Strategically simple sites with meticulous coding. How can I help you?

As a freelancer, I enjoy working with entrepreneurs, schools, churches and other non-profits. I can start from scratch or update your existing site to meet the demands of our mobile culture. If a user-friendly website builder is best for you, I can help you get started with some hands-on training. Let’s talk.
